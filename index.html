Pioneering Social Impact with Innovation

Welcome to the Tech for Impact Summit hosted by Socious
This groundbreaking event brings together international and Japanese speakers from government, tech companies, academia, and more to explore real-world applications of innovation for social and environmental impact. Explore interactive panels and workshops on innovations in global health and telecommunications to insights into civic enterprise and regulation and dive deep into the intersection of technology and social good.
